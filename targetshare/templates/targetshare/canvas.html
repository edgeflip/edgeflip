What is Targeted Sharing?

Targeted Sharing is a digital advocacy tool that helps Facebook users find causes and organizations they care about, discover new opportunities for volunteering and community engagement, and connect with friends who share similar passions. And while it puts the latest in data science and machine learning at your fingertips, it's based on the most old-fashioned technology there is: real social networks coming together to make a difference in the world.
